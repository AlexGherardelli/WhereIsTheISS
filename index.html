International Space Station (ISS) is a large spacecraft that orbits around the Earth. ISS is like a home for a crew of astronauts where they can stay. It travels at an average speed of 27,600 kilometers per hour (17,150 mph). Whereas ISS completes 15.54 orbits per day, so it takes approximately 92 minutes to complete one orbit around the earth.
